Maple Syrup Baked Salmon

Ingredients:

  • salmon fillet
  • maple syrup
  • cooking wine
  • soy sauce
  • grapeseed oil
What else is needed:
  • drinking water
  • big bowl
  • paper towel
  • aluminum foil
  • oven pan
  • oven
  • oven mitts

Instructions

  1. Use drinking water to gently wash all sides of the salmon fillet twice.
    No need to use hands to wash, just use the water to run over the salmon.
  2. Get rid of remaining water by using paper towel to wrap around the salmon.
  3. Remove paper towel and place salmon in a big bowl.
  4. Cover salmon with maple syrup.
  5. Add cooking wine and soy sauce.
    Ratio of sauces should be 2 maple syrup:1 cooking wine:1 soy sauce
  6. Make sure the sauce is evenly covering the salmon and leave it there to settle for at least 15 minutes.
  7. Flip the salmon over and let the sauce settle in for another 15+ minutes.
  8. Place aluminum foil over oven pan and twist each corner up if the pan isn't deep enough.
  9. Add a tiny bit of grapeseed oil into the oven pan.
  10. Place salmon fillet onto the oven pan and pour remaining sauces from the bowl into the oven pan.
  11. Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
  12. Place oven pan containing salmon fillet into pre-heated oven and bake for 20 minutes.
  13. Once time is up, take oven pan out using oven mitts.
  14. Enjoy the delicious maple syrup salmon dish.
